chopped salad

description

Italian dressing, crisp veggies and Parmesan cheese combine into one extraordinary salad.

ingredients

  • italian dressing of your liking
  • romaine heart
  • cucumber
  • cherry tomatoes
  • sliced pepperoncini
  • parmesian shavings
  • shallot
  • green olives
  1. Chop romaine and lettuce. Thinly slice the shallot. Peel and chop cucumber. Slice tomatoes and olives in half.
  2. Mix together all the ingredients for the salad, including the dressing, and toss to combine. If making in advance, refrigerate the components separately; bring the dressing to room temperature before serving.
